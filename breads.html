Banana Bread


Ingredients

  • 3 medium ripe bananas mashed (may let sit a couple of hours to darken)
  • 1/2 cup Canola oil
  • 1 cup Sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp of Vanilla
  • 2 cups Flour
  • 1 tsp Baking Soda

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Mix oil, sugar, eggs, vanilla
  3. Add bananas then flour and soda
  4. Put in greased loaf pan
  5. Bake for 1 hour

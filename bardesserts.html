Indoor S'mores


Ingredients

  • 8 cups (13-ounce box) graham cracker cereal
  • 6 cups (10.5-ounce bag) miniature marshmallows, divided
  • 1 1/2 cups (9 ounces) NESTLÉ® TOLL HOUSE® Milk Chocolate Morsels
  • 5 tbsp butter or margarine, cut into pieces
  • 1/4 cup light corn syrup (optional)
  • 1 tsp vanilla

Directions

  1. Grease 13 x 9-inch baking pan. Pour cereal into large bowl.
  2. Heat 5 cups marshmallows, morsels, butter and corn syrup in medium, heavy-duty saucepan over low heat, stirring constantly until smooth. Remove from heat. Stir in vanilla extract.
  3. Pour marshmallow mixture over cereal; stir until well coated. Stir in remaining marshmallows. Press mixture into prepared pan. Refrigerate for 1 hour or until firm. Cut into bars.

Microwave Method

  • Microwave 5 cups marshmallows, morsels, butter and corn syrup in large, microwave-safe bowl on HIGH (100%) power for 2 to 3 1/2 minutes, stirring every minute, until smooth. Stir in vanilla extract. Proceed as above.

Oatmeal Fudge Bars

Ingredients

Fudge

  • 1(12 oz)pkg chocolate chips
  • 1(14 oz)can sweetened condensed milk
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 2 tsp vanilla

Oatmeal Cookie

  • 1 1/2 cups brown sugar
  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 tbsp vanilla
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 cups flour
  • 3 cups old fashion oats

Directions

Preheat oven to 350°F

  1. Melt first three ingredients(chips, milk, butter) together. Remove from heat; add vanilla. Set aside.
  2. Mix butter, brown sugar, vanilla, salt, and eggs together. Add soda and flour.
  3. Stir in oats by hand. Pat 2/3 of oatmeal mixture on to pan.
  4. Pour melted chocolate filling on top.
  5. Sprinkle remaining oatmeal mixture on top.
  6. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes

Peppermint Oreo Dessert

Ingredients

  • 1 pkg gf Oreos (usually better not double stuffed or you can use an Oreo pie crust)
  • 1 big cool whip
  • 1 10oz bag of mini marshmallow
  • 12 crushed peppermint Candy Canes

Directions

  1. Crush Oreos and mix with 3 - 4 tbs melted butter
  2. Press in 9x13 cake pan
  3. Refrigerate over night
  4. Mix cool whip, crushed candy canes, and marshmallows together and dump in Oreo crust
